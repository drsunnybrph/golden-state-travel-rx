The Story

Dr. Sunny B, PharmD

Licensed California Pharmacist · Travel Medicine

Dr. Sunny B, PharmD, is a licensed California pharmacist — a Doctor of Pharmacy, not a medical doctor — whose clinical background is in emergency medicine. Golden State Travel Rx is his independent travel-health practice. Years on the front line of emergency care taught him how often a preventable problem abroad becomes a serious one at home. Travel medicine is the answer: a short, focused consultation that gets travelers the right guidance and the right medications before they leave.

The practice operates as telehealth for patients across California, with deep roots in Solano County (Fairfield, Vacaville, Suisun City) and Ventura County (Simi Valley, Oxnard, Thousand Oaks). Consultations are available in English, Punjabi, and Hindi — serving the South Asian diaspora and the VFR (Visiting Friends & Relatives) travel market, a high-risk corridor that benefits from culturally fluent, destination-specific care.

Travel medications are furnished under independent California pharmacist authority — Business & Professions Code § 4052(a)(10)(A)(3) and 16 CCR § 1746.5. This means eligible CDC-recommended travel medications that don't require a diagnosis can be furnished directly, with no separate physician visit and no collaborative practice agreement. Your primary care provider is notified within 14 days of any medication furnished, as the regulation requires.

Beyond travel, I also furnish naloxone — the opioid-overdose rescue medication — under California's statewide protocol (BPC § 4052.01 and 16 CCR § 1746.3). It comes straight out of my time in the emergency department: a simple kit at home protects young people after a night out and older adults managing pain on several medications alike. Scope & Authority

Travel-medication furnishing is performed within California pharmacist scope under BPC § 4052(a)(10) and 16 CCR § 1746.5. Naloxone is furnished under the statewide protocol pursuant to BPC § 4052.01 and 16 CCR § 1746.3. Both are backed by completed immunization, travel-medicine, and opioid-antagonist training and ongoing continuing education.

Multilingual & Cultural Competency

Consultations in English, Punjabi, and Hindi (oral fluency), with particular focus on travelers visiting South Asia, including India and Pakistan — a high-risk VFR corridor that needs tailored prophylaxis and counseling.

Who I Serve

Telehealth for any patient physically located in California. Strongest local roots in Solano County (Fairfield, Vacaville, Suisun City, Vallejo) and Ventura County (Simi Valley, Oxnard, Thousand Oaks, Camarillo).
