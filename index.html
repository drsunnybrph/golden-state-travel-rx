One Focus, Done Right

Pre-Travel Care, Pharmacist-Led

A focused travel medicine practice — destination risk assessment, vaccine counseling, and prescription travel medications furnished directly under California pharmacist authority. No referral, no clinic wait.

Destination Risk Assessment

A clinical pre-travel review built on the CDC Yellow Book — malaria zones, food & water safety, altitude, insect-borne risk, and a written plan tailored to your exact itinerary.

Travel Medication Furnishing

CDC-recommended travel medications that don't require a diagnosis — furnished independently under BPC § 4052(a)(10) and sent electronically to the pharmacy of your choice. No CPA, no MD visit.

Vaccine Counseling & Referral

Which vaccines you actually need, how far in advance, and where to get them. Required immunizations are referred to a designated local clinic for administration.

Multilingual & VFR-Aware

Consultations in English, Punjabi, and Hindi — with particular experience in South Asia travel corridors (India, Pakistan), a high-risk VFR route that needs tailored prophylaxis.

Naloxone Furnishing

Opioid-overdose rescue medication, furnished without a prescription under California's statewide protocol — BPC § 4052.01 and 16 CCR § 1746.3. The required safe-use consultation is included, then sent to your pharmacy.

How Furnishing Works

From Your Laptop to Your Pharmacy

California law lets a trained pharmacist independently furnish CDC-recommended travel medications that don't require a diagnosis. After your consult, eligible medications are transmitted electronically to your chosen pharmacy — CVS, Walgreens, Costco, or your local independent — for pickup. You never need a separate doctor's visit.

Travel medications are furnished under independent pharmacist authority pursuant to California Business & Professions Code § 4052(a)(10)(A)(3) and 16 CCR § 1746.5. Naloxone is furnished pursuant to the statewide protocol under California Business & Professions Code § 4052.01 and 16 CCR § 1746.3, which includes a mandatory consultation that cannot be waived. No collaborative practice agreement required. Furnishing is limited to CDC-recommended travel medications that do not require a diagnosis and to naloxone under the statewide protocol. Services are available to patients physically located in California. No on-site vaccinations; required immunizations are referred to a local clinic.
