Trip-planning content, safety data (U.S. Department of State), and health notices (U.S. CDC Travelers' Health) are provided for general educational purposes and are not individualized medical, legal, or travel advice. Advisory levels and health notices change frequently; always confirm the official source before you travel. Travel medications are furnished under independent pharmacist authority pursuant to California Business & Professions Code § 4052(a)(10)(A)(3) and 16 CCR § 1746.5. Naloxone is furnished pursuant to the statewide protocol under California Business & Professions Code § 4052.01 and 16 CCR § 1746.3. Furnishing is limited to CDC-recommended travel medications that do not require a diagnosis and to naloxone under the statewide protocol. Services are available to patients physically located in California. No on-site vaccinations; required immunizations are referred to a local clinic. Not a substitute for emergency care or your primary care provider.
